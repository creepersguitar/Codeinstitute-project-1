The first lifehack

Are you doing renovations for a room where you live?

Well if you are then why not a bin bag to the floor it will help with an easy clean up.


The Second lifehack

Is your favourite candle getting low?

Does your lighter not reach?

Well if you have answered yes to both of them, just grab a piece of pasta. If you light a piece of uncooked spaghetti, you can reach the wick easier.


The third lifehack

Do you ever wake up to an alarm in the morning, turn it off and then just fall back asleep?

Well if you do then just put your alarm clock, or phone on the opposite side of the room. That way it should force you to get out of bed and go turn it off.


The fourth lifehack

Now a lifehack for those of you who are working from home.

If you are working from home, and you have a break to help you eyes. Just find one item to sort out or to clean. Your body will appreciate it considerably.
